Discover Hawaiian Aloha

Featured at the Obama National Park is the majestic Wailua Falls on the Wailua River, the only navigable river in the Hawaiian Islands.

The river’s fresh water pours down from Mount Wai‘ale‘ale, which is one of the wettest spots on the planet.
